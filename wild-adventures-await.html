Wild Adventures Await

Embark on a thrilling educational journey at Woodcroft Wildspace, where the wonders of nature come alive in the heart of Enfield. Our entrance at Downes Court, N21 3PT, is your gateway to a world of excitement and learning.

Join us seven days a week from 9am to 5.30pm for an immersive experience like no other. From school visits to engaging activities for groups like Cubs, Beavers, and Brownies, there's something wild for everyone at Woodcroft Wildspace.



Become a part of our mission to support the Duke of Edinburgh's Award Scheme through student volunteers who lead daring wildspace projects. At Woodcroft, adventure and education blend seamlessly to create unforgettable memories.
