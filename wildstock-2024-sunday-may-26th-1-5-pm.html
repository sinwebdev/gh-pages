WILDSTOCK 2024 SUNDAY MAY 26th 1-5 pm

May 1, 2024 News & Events

 
Wildstock is back!  Yes, Wildstock '24 is gearing up for another fun-packed musical extravaganza. Bring a picnic and chill in the beautiful meadow in Woodcroft Wildspace. Enjoy the craft beers and wines courtesy of The Little Green Dragon while listening to a selection of local musicians throughout the afternoon.
Tickets available from https://buytickets.at/enfieldchaserotaryclub/1201210

All funds raised go to local charities - Little Sparks Enfield, Cooking Champions, and Woodcroft Wildspace - all fantastic local causes.

Location - Woodcroft Wildspace N21 3PT
