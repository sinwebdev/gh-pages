Our Mission

Welcome to the Educational Wildspace of Enfield, a sanctuary where nature thrives and minds come alive!

Located in Downes Court, N21 3PT, our gates are open 7 days a week from 9am to 5.30pm, (4pm winter) inviting all wild souls to immerse in the wonders of the wild.

Join us in supporting school visits, engaging with Cubs, Beavers, and Brownies, and championing the Duke of Edinburgh’s Award Scheme through thrilling wildspace projects led by our Woodcroft volunteers.
